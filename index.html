Privacy Policy

Last updated: 3/10/2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to InspireMe Quotes. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our mobile application.

2. Data Collection

InspireMe Quotes is designed as an offline-first application. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, usage statistics, or analytics to our servers. All data, including your saved quotes and preferences, is stored locally on your device.

3. Permissions

Our app may request the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To send you daily quote reminders (if enabled).
  • Storage: To save your preferences and custom quotes locally.

4. Third-Party Services

Because this app operates entirely offline, it does not integrate with third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising services that collect user data.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the app store support page.
